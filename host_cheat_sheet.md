# HOST CHEAT SHEET — 34 PLAYER TRAITORS

## QUICK STRUCTURE OVERVIEW

Start: 34 Players  
Traitors: 6 (3 per house)  
Houses: A (17) / B (17)

### PHASE 1 — HOUSES (≈2 Hours)

Run 3 full rotations (A then B).

Each House Cycle:
- 5 min Challenge
- 10 min Debate
- 5 min Vote
- 2 min Night Kill

Each cycle removes:
- 1 Banishment
- 1 Murder

After 3 rotations:
34 → 22

---

## PHASE 2 — MERGE (≈1.5 Hours)

At 22 Players:
- Merge announcement
- Traitors now all know each other
- If fewer than 3 Traitors remain → Recruit 1

### MERGE ROUND 1
- Dagger Challenge
- Chalice Ritual
- Double Banishment
- Night Kill (or Chalice Mark kill)

22 → 19

### MERGE ROUNDS 2–5
Each:
- 10 min Debate
- 1 Banishment
- 1 Murder

Stop murders at 11 players.

---

## ENDGAME

From 11 → 6:
Banishments only.

At 6:
Final Circle vote to END or CONTINUE.

---

## CRITICAL RULES

- 30 sec speaking limit.
- No whispering.
- No phones except night.
- Eliminated players silent.
- Host decisions final.
